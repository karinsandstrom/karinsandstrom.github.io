About Me

I received my Ph.D. in 2009 from the University of California, Berkeley. I then moved to Heidelberg, Germany as a postdoctoral researcher in the Galaxies and Cosmology Department. In 2011, I was awarded a Marie Curie fellowship from the European Union. I moved to University of Arizona in 2013 to become the Bok Postdoctoral Fellow. In 2015, I will start as an assistant professor at UCSD.
