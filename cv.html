Research Interests

  • Multiwavelength studies of the interstellar medium in nearby galaxies. ISM phases; heating and cooling of gas and dust; feedback from stellar populations; chemical enrichment.
  • Star formation efficiency and the properties of molecular gas. The influence of galactic environment on molecular gas abundance and properties.
  • The life cycle of ISM dust. Formation and destruction of dust; variations of dust properties as a function of metallicity; the impact of dust on ISM physics.
